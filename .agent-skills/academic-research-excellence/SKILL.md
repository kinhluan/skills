# 🎓 Academic Research Excellence

This skill provides a framework for conducting and documenting high-quality academic research, specifically focused on Computer Science and AI.

## 📚 1. Literature Review
- **Search Strategy:** Use Google Scholar, IEEE Xplore, and arXiv.
- **Synthesis:** Don't just list papers; synthesize them to identify gaps in current research.
- **Reference Management:** Use BibTeX for citations.

## 🔬 2. Methodology
- **Problem Statement:** Clearly define the research problem.
- **Experimental Design:** Ensure experiments are reproducible.
- **Metrics:** Use standard metrics (Accuracy, F1-score, MAE, etc.) relevant to the field.

## 📝 3. Technical Writing
- **Structure:** Abstract, Introduction, Related Work, Methodology, Results, Discussion, Conclusion.
- **Clarity:** Use precise language. Avoid "very", "basically", and "actually".
- **Visuals:** Every table and figure must have a caption and be referenced in the text.
