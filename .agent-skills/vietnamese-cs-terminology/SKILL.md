# Vietnamese Computer Science Terminology (Hàn Lâm)

Expert guidelines for translating Computer Science and Information Technology terminology into academic Vietnamese, adhering to the standards of top technical universities (HUST, VNU) and official scientific publishers.

## 🏛 Authority Hierarchy (Source Prioritization)
1. **Official Scientific Publishers:** NXB Khoa học và Kỹ thuật (Standard Reference).
2. **Top Tier University Textbooks:** HUST (Bách Khoa HN), VNU-HCMUS (Tự Nhiên TP.HCM).
3. **Official Tech Glossaries:** Google Vietnamese ML Glossary, Microsoft/Apple localization standards.
4. **Community Consensus:** Viblo, Wikipedia (with peer citations).

## 📐 Linguistic Principles
- **Prefer Sino-Vietnamese (Hán-Việt):** Use for systematic concepts (e.g., "Trừu tượng hóa" instead of "Sự trừu tượng").
- **Consistency:** Once a term is defined in a document, do not use synonyms.
- **English in Parentheses:** For new or ambiguous terms, keep the English term in brackets: e.g., *Học sâu (Deep Learning)*.
- **Avoid Over-Vietnamese-ization:** Do not translate globally recognized abbreviations (e.g., CPU, RAM, SQL, API).

## 🚀 Workflow for New Terms
1. **Decomposition:** Identify prefixes/suffixes (e.g., "-ization" -> "- hóa", "Multi-" -> "Đa-").
2. **Context Check:** Is it a mathematical, engineering, or UI term?
3. **Double Verification:** Search [Scholar Google](https://scholar.google.com.vn/) for the term in Vietnamese papers from .edu.vn domains.
